Grid Home — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Data we collect

  • Step count — stored locally on your device only, never transmitted
  • App usage statistics — stored locally on your device only, never transmitted

Data we do NOT collect

Third party services

None of these services receive personal data from you.
